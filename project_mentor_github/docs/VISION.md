# Vision

Project Mentor exists because human potential is far greater than our current education systems are able to unlock.

Education is often constrained by time, classroom size, money, geography, and standardized pacing. Those constraints do not exist because teachers do not care. They exist because one person can only help so many learners at once.

Project Mentor is not meant to replace teachers.

It is meant to give every learner a patient, adaptive practice partner that helps between lessons, at home, in classrooms, and during moments when a person would otherwise be stuck alone.

## Mission

Build AI mentors that help every person discover what they are capable of learning.

## What We Are Solving

We are not solving “people do not know piano.”

We are solving:

> People struggle to learn because education cannot adapt to every individual.

Music is the first proof.

## What Success Looks Like

A learner sits down confused, stuck, or intimidated.

The mentor observes what they are doing, identifies the real obstacle, explains it in a way that fits them, gives them a small challenge, encourages them through mistakes, and helps them leave more capable than when they started.

## North Star

The AI should never try to prove how intelligent it is.

It should help the learner feel intelligent.
